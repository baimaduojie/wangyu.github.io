About Me

My scholarly endeavors are centered on the cutting-edge intersection of educational cognitive neuroscience, STEM education, collaborative learning, and AI+ education.

My expertise is characterized by proficient use of functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS) to unravel the complexities of learning processes and cognitive development in diverse educational settings.
